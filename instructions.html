The best way to use the AI! Simply highlight any text on any website, right-click it, and choose "Ask Imoji about..." from the menu. A powerful popup will appear with your answer!

  • If the server is sleeping, you'll see an error. Just click the "Retry" button to wake it up!
  • When the answer appears, use the "Copy Text" button to save it to your clipboard instantly.

Alternative: The Text Command

In any text box, you can still type :ai, a space, your question, and then press the spacebar twice at the end.

Example: :ai tell me a fun fact about Japan
